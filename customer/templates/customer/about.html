About Us

Established in 2017, Delicious is a locally-owned, independent catering and restaurant service.

Four years later, we’re still happily serving the University Students with quality fresh food. Our catering service has grown to become one of Pantnagar's largest and most recognized services.

  • It’s simple: “YUM!” is always our goal. Because everything you eat should be packed with flavor and full of joy.
  • Food has that magic ability of bringing people together to laugh, to celebrate, to connect — and no one should ever be excluded.
  • We promise to always push ourselves to offer the best food possible, again and again.

We’re transforming what it means to be convenient and delicious. Every product we offer — today and tomorrow — is crafted with fresh, best-quality, Rich, and healthy ingredients. Because everyone deserves delicious food made with love.
